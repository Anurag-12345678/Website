About Us
SHRI GURUDEV AGRO INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED

SHRI GURUDEV AGRO INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED is a Private company incorporated on 06 Dec 2021. It is classified as Non-government company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Chhattisgarh.


At Shri Gurudev Agro, we are committed to delivering high-quality rice through modern milling techniques and deep-rooted agricultural expertise. Established with a vision to support farmers and provide consumers with the finest rice, our mill combines traditional values with advanced processing technology. Located in the heart of a rich agricultural region, we focus on quality, cleanliness, and sustainability at every stage. Our goal is to build long-term relationships with our customers by ensuring purity, nutrition, and trust in every grain.

Directors of SHRI GURUDEV AGRO INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED are Mr. RAMESH JAIN and Mr. MAYUR JAIN.




A parboiled rice mill is a specialized facility that processes raw paddy into parboiled rice, a type of rice that is partially boiled in the husk before milling.


The parboiling process enhances the nutritional value, improves the texture, and increases the shelf life of the rice. It involves three main stages: soaking, steaming, and drying, followed by the standard milling process to remove the husk and bran layers.


Parboiled rice is widely consumed in many parts of the world due to its firmness, reduced stickiness, and higher resistance to insect infestation. With growing demand for healthier and more durable rice products, parboiled rice mills play a crucial role in the agricultural and food processing sectors.
